## Features
- **Custom Data Structures**:
  - Doubly Linked List (`DblList`) for efficient queue operations.
  - Queue (`ListQueue`) for managing customers in line.
- **Simulation**:
  - Handles customer service in three offices (Registrar, Cashier, Financial Aid).
  - Tracks service times, idle times, and office transitions.
- **Scalable Design**:
  - Easily extendable to support more offices or different service rules.
